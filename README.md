# School-Bus-Tracking-Application

This project is a real time school bus tracking and student safety application designed to give parents and students peace of mind during daily commutes. By integrating onboard GPS telematics with student scanning hardware, the platform ensures parents always know exactly where the school bus is and precisely when their students boards or exits.

Key Features:

*Real-Time Fleet Telematics: Streams live GPS coordinates from onboard bus tracking devices directly to the user application.

*Active Commute Window: Parents and students can monitor live bus locations and receive ETAs only when the student is assigned to  or currently on a route.

*Dual-Point Scan Verification: Students scan an ID
badge or device upon boarding and exiting the bus (for both morning and afternoon routes).

*Instant Parent Notifications: Automated 'ping' alerts are sent to parent devices the moment a student scans on or off the vehicle, establishing a secure chain of custody from stop to stop.
